Faq

What should I do while waiting for you to arrive?

Don’t panic — we’ve done this thousands of times and you’re in good hands. Move valuables away from the affected area, open windows for airflow where safe, and avoid household fans as they can spread contaminants.

Do I need to leave my home during the work?

In most cases, no. Our equipment runs quietly and safely while you go about your day. If you feel it's safer to temporarily relocate, just let us know so we can coordinate.

Can you prevent mould from developing?

Yes — fast response is key. Mould can start within 24–48 hours of water damage. Our equipment is designed to bring moisture levels down before mould takes hold. If pre-existing spores are present, water damage accelerates their growth — which is why acting quickly matters.

Will you speak to my insurance company for me?

Whilst we handle insurance claims daily and know exactly what documentation insurers need, we cannot look after your claim on your behalf. We can provide detailed reports, photos, and moisture readings, and we're happy to share these directly with your insurer to make the process as stress-free as possible.

What if I am renting and do not own the property?

We work with renters regularly. Simply let your landlord or property manager know about the damage, and we can coordinate directly with them or their insurer. Your contents may be covered under your renters insurance—we can help document any personal items affected.

How long does the process take to dry out?

Every type of situation is different. It depends on how much water has been absorbed by the porous materials inside your home.

Are you available 24/7 including public holidays?

Yes, water damage doesn't wait and neither do we. Our team is available around the clock, 365 days a year—including Christmas, Easter, and every public holiday. When you ask for help, we will dispatch a team immediately.
